Disney Animation Center
SCI-Arc (2018)

Architecture
Rhino

Media and content are arguably the most abundant product of our time. The Disney Animation Center – designed for one of the most recognizable media corporations of the 20th and 21st century - acknowledges our desire for the consumption of media. The animation center offers creative office and exhibition space for the production of content, while simultaneously providing the canvas for which the media is displayed upon and made consumable to the public.

This project was completed in collaboration with Fateme Jalali.
