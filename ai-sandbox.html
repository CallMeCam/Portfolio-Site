Unreal Engine

AI Sandbox is an attempt at synthesizing machine learning and digital modeling within the context of architecture. Led by Damjan Jovanovic and supported by SCI-Arc and Epic, this MegaGrant project made use of Unreal Engine’s 3D modeling capabilities while incorporating user action-based machine learning to provide real time suggestions and assistance to the user.

Problem

The project “...takes the proceduralist ‘blue foam’ approach to form making into the realm of AI to amplify and extend the ideation phase and automate the development phase. The model learns the tendencies and style of designers over time and can simultaneously propose insight into multiple resolved models” (source↗).

I served as an early-stage developer on the project, contributing to much of the platform’s design and implementation of Unreal Engine’s modeling features and the pgrogam's UX and UI. One aim of the project was to set itself apart from conventional modeling software design through unique human-computer interactions.

Audit

To understand the conventional modeling software that a majority of architects use, I examined six platforms including: Revit, Blender, Cinema 4D, Maya, Rhino, and AutoCAD. Identifying commonalities and shared attributes across all was a primary goal of the audit. I found that all six of the platforms commonly relied on small tool icons, toolbar ribbons, ‘project outlines’ or browsers, ‘properties’ panels, and sub-menus (menus within menus). In order to set itself apart from this, AI Sandbox aimed to incorporate features like large and descriptive tool buttons, a simplified menu flow, and a supplemental radial tool menu for easy access to important features.

Ideation

Following the survey, we ideated on what our own user experience should encompass. With AI Sandbox situating itself at the early stages of modeling, we approached the program as a “shallow” platform with a strategically limited tool set and a simplified interface. Sketches, like those shown here, were my primary way of exploring what this would look like.
