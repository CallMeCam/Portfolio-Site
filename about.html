Cameron McCormick⭧ received a BFA with emphasis in Architectural Design from James Madison University in 2017, and a Master in Architecture from SCI-Arc in 2020.

I consider myself a design generalist with interests spanning most facets of design, from architecture to content creation. I have strong interests in designing experiences and tools. My work has been described as atypical, and that’s exactly what I try to achieve. By recognizing ‘initial solutions’, we can work beyond them to arrive at something unique, and ideally, greater.

What I enjoy most is ideating, innovating, and making discoveries throughout the design process.

These are some things I think I'm good at:

  • Working in/with design systems
  • Solving complex and/or nuanced problems
  • Thinking in networks
  • Thinking in space
  • Thinking beyond inital concepts
  • Working with a team/in a partnership
  • Learning new tools
